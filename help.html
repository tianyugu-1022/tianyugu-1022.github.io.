Mental Health Resources at UNC-Chapel Hill

1. UNC CAPS (Counseling and Psychological Services)

Role: The main mental health service for UNC students.

Services: Individual therapy, group therapy, crisis services, medication management, referrals, and same-day walk-ins for urgent concerns.

Website: https://caps.unc.edu/

Location: James A. Taylor Student Health Building, 3rd Floor

Phone: 919-966-3658 (24/7 phone support available)

2. Heels Care Network

What it is: A comprehensive online hub for mental health and well-being resources.

Services: Self-help tools, peer support options, and connections to professional care.

Website:https://care.unc.edu

3. Student Wellness

What it is: Provides programs and workshops to promote mental health, resilience, and coping strategies.

Services: Stress management, mindfulness training, peer support programs.

Website: https://studentwellness.unc.edu

4. TimelyCare (Virtual Mental Health Support)

What it is: Free virtual mental health care for UNC students, available 24/7.

Services: Counseling sessions, health coaching, and self-care tools.

Website: https://timelycare.com/

5. Dean of Students Office

What it is: Offers academic and personal support for students facing challenges, including mental health struggles.

Services: Case management, advocacy, and help navigating campus resources.

Website: https://dos.unc.edu

6. Peer Support Programs

Peer2Peer: A student-led program where trained peers provide confidential support for fellow students experiencing mental health challenges.

Website: https://studentwellness.unc.edu/involvement/join-peer-group/

7. Carolina Recovery Program (CRP)

What it is: Supports students in recovery from substance use disorders, which can co-occur with depression.

Website: https://studentwellness.unc.edu/programs/carolina-recovery-program/

8. Academic Accommodations (Accessibility Resources & Service - ARS)

What it is: Helps students with mental health conditions (like depression) access academic accommodations.

Website: https://ars.unc.edu
